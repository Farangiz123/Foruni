About me

I am an ordinary girl, who is an art enthusiast, loves kpop, takes the trash out every other day, helps her mom with chores, and does little business. Moreover, I love taking the initiative and have led teams to solve problems and connected everyone with my visions.

As a kid, I always wanted to make the world a better place for everyone to live in. Ever since then, I have been helping people. In search of turning my determination into reality, I went through a beautiful journey. My journey so far has been a series of curves; at each, I've learned something new and understood myself better.

Along the way, I've learned to embrace the process, think holistically, collaborate in a team, and empathize with the people around me. I've dived into multiple projects and gained a deeper understanding of all four.

I spend my free time reading, writing, doing karate, and enjoying art masterpieces. I love getting outside my comfort zone! I want to grow and explore the policymaking field with experts and help people not only in my country but worldwide.
